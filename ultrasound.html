ULTRASOUND

Ultrasound is a high-frequency sound that you cannot hear but it can be emitted and detected by special machines. It travels freely through fluid and soft tissues and is a safe and painless scan. Our procedures conform to world-class standards and PNDT guidelines.

The sonography test works on the concept of “sonar” technology, sending high-frequency sound waves to the part of the body being scanned. If there are no anomalies or blockages in the target organ or soft tissue being tested, the sound waves will travel smoothly through them. In case, there are certain blockages or abnormalities, the sound waves will bounce off and generate dense images.

At Diagnato Diagnostic Centre, we specialise in a wide range of ultrasound procedures that include:

  • Abdominal ultrasound
  • Musculoskeletal ultrasound
  • Thyroid ultrasound
  • Breast ultrasound
  • Prostate ultrasound
  • Pregnancy ultrasound

    • Purpose Of An Ultrasound

    Your doctor may recommend an ultrasound test for various reasons as discussed herewith:

  • Check the ovaries and uterus during pregnancy and continuously monitor the health and growth of the developing baby
  • Examine a lump in the breast
  • Detect a disease of the gallbladder
  • Evaluate the thyroid gland
  • Assess metabolic bone diseases or musculoskeletal conditions
  • Diagnose prostate and genital problems
  • Examine joint inflammation
  • Determine if blood is flowing optimally
  • Guide a biopsy procedure or treatment for a tumor
