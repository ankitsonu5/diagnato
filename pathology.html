    • Why Do You Need a Pathology Test?

    There are several reasons why your doctor may recommend getting a pathology test. Visit our diagnostic centre to:

  • Diagnose various health conditions through blood, urine, or tissue samples
  • Monitor the effectiveness of ongoing treatments
  • Identify infections, diseases, and other abnormalities in the body
  • Evaluate organ function and overall health status
  • Detect early signs of diseases like diabetes, cancer, and thyroid disorders
  • Assess nutritional status and deficiencies
  • Screen for genetic conditions or predispositions

    • The results from pathology tests are highly accurate and can help diagnose a wide range of health issues, guide treatment plans, and monitor the effectiveness of ongoing treatments.

    Pathology Test Procedure

    At Diagnato Diagnostic Centre, we ensure high levels of patient care, safety, and comfort during the pathology testing process. Our procedures are designed to be as comfortable and convenient as possible.

    Depending on the specific test, you may be required to provide a blood sample, urine sample, or other bodily fluids. Our trained technicians will ensure that the sample collection is performed in a hygienic and minimally invasive manner. In some cases, you may need to fast or follow specific instructions before the test.

    After the sample is collected, it will be analyzed in our state-of-the-art laboratory. The entire process usually takes about 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the test. After the analysis, an experienced pathologist will review the results and prepare a detailed report, highlighting any abnormalities or findings.
