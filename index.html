WELCOME

to Diagnate Diagnostic Center, Bodkarpur, Jaunpur

A vision to strengthen healthcare in the communities we serve and empower patients to make informed choices, was at the genesis of Diagnostic & Medical Research Institute.

Inaugurated in January 2000, the Center is a significant social initiative from Diagnostic Group. It is designed to raise India's global standing as a healthcare destination, with emphasis on excellence in clinical services, diagnostic facilities and research activities.

We are the only Diagnostic center in Jaunpur to function with a FULL TIME SPECIALIST SYSTEM, that ensures the availability and access to the best medical talent around-the-clock.
