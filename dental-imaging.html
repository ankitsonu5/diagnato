    • Why Do You Need Dental Imaging?

    There are several reasons why your dentist may recommend getting a dental imaging test. Visit our diagnostic centre to:

  • Diagnose tooth decay and cavities
  • Identify gum disease and its severity
  • Assess the condition of wisdom teeth
  • Detect abnormalities in the jawbone
  • Evaluate the need for orthodontic treatment
  • Identify cysts, tumors, or other abnormalities in the mouth
  • Monitor the progress of dental treatments

    • The results from dental imaging are highly accurate and can help diagnose dental health problems, guide treatment plans, and monitor the effectiveness of ongoing treatments.

    Dental Imaging Procedure

    At Diagnato Diagnostic Centre, we ensure high levels of patient care, safety, and comfort. Therefore, we leave no stone unturned to ensure the procedure is performed while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

    Before the procedure, you may be asked to remove any metal objects from your mouth, such as dentures or jewelry. During the imaging, you will be required to bite down on a small plastic piece while the imaging equipment captures detailed images of your teeth and jaw. The process is painless and non-invasive, and it typically takes only a few minutes.

    After the imaging, an experienced radiologist will analyze the results and prepare a detailed report, highlighting any abnormalities in your dental health.
