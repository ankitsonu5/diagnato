Welcome

to Charak Diagnostic Center, Chowk

A vision to strengthen healthcare in the communities we serve and empower patients to make informed choices, was at the genesis of Charak Diagnostic & Medical Research Institute.

Inaugurated in January 2000, the Center is a significant social initiative from Charak Group. It is designed to raise India's global standing as a healthcare destination, with emphasis on excellence in clinical services, diagnostic facilities and research activities.

We are the only Diagnostic center in Lucknow to function with a FULL TIME SPECIALIST SYSTEM, that ensures the availability and access to the best medical talent around-the-clock.

Our Vision

To be nationally respected for excellence in patient care and most trusted for personalized coordinated care of the patients.

Our Mission

To improve the health and healing of the people and communities we serve at affordable price.
