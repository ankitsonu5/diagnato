About Our Diagnostic Centre

Welcome to Diagnato Diagnostic Center, where advanced technology meets compassionate care. Located in Bodkarpur, Jaunpur, we are dedicated to providing high-quality diagnostic services with a focus on precision, reliability, and patient satisfaction.

At Diagnato, we understand that accurate diagnostics are crucial for effective treatment and overall health. That’s why we employ the latest imaging and testing technologies to deliver precise results and support your health journey. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with cutting-edge equipment, including MRI, CT Scan, Ultrasound, and more, ensuring comprehensive diagnostic capabilities. Our team of highly skilled and experienced medical professionals is committed to delivering personalized care with empathy and expertise.
