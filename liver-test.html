Liver Function Test

At Diagnato Diagnostic Centre, we offer comprehensive Liver Function Tests (LFTs) to evaluate the health of your liver. These tests measure the levels of enzymes, proteins, and substances produced or processed by the liver. Monitoring your liver function is essential for diagnosing liver conditions, monitoring the progression of liver disease, and guiding treatment decisions.

Liver Function Tests typically include measurements of enzymes like ALT (Alanine Aminotransferase) and AST (Aspartate Aminotransferase), as well as bilirubin, albumin, and other vital components. These tests provide crucial information about how well your liver is performing its essential functions, such as detoxifying harmful substances, metabolizing drugs, and producing proteins critical for blood clotting.
