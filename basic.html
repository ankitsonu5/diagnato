About The Basic Health Checkup

A basic health checkup provides a comprehensive assessment of your overall health and helps in the early detection of potential health issues. It typically includes a range of essential tests, such as a Complete Blood Count (CBC) to evaluate general health, a Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP) to assess metabolic function and kidney health, a Lipid Profile to monitor cholesterol levels, Urinalysis to check for urinary and kidney problems, a Chest X-ray to visualize heart and lung conditions, and a Physical Examination to identify any signs of health issues. This thorough evaluation helps in maintaining optimal health and preventing future complications.

What are the Benefits of Basic Health Checkup?

A basic health checkup helps in early detection and prevention of health issues, ensuring timely treatment. It provides a comprehensive overview of your overall health status and identifies potential risks. Regular checkups lead to better management of existing conditions and improved long-term health outcomes.
