Diagnato Diagnostic Centre provides excellent workflow and image quality to drive throughput and confident diagnoses while ensuring patient satisfaction. Our unwavering commitment to innovation has helped us integrate cutting-edge technology and achieve greater heights in patient care.

Best quality X-Rays using the lowest radiation dose

At Diagnato Diagnostic Centre, has a wide range of Digital X-Ray systems, which are a mark of the global quality that we strive to provide. We have the world’s most advanced X-Ray system, the Definium 8000 by GE Healthcare, and the latest in DR technology, the Wireless DR System by FujiFilm Medical Systems & Allengers Medical Systems. All routine plain X-Rays and special procedures, including barium studies, and intravenous urography. HSG, MCU, etc., are conducted using these systems.

The X-Ray passes through the patient's body and the digital camera (located on the other side of the patient) and captures the resulting image. The main base station stores the pictures on a hard disk for image retrieval and processing.

What is X-Ray?

X-Rays are a form of electromagnetic radiation used to create images of the inside of the body. They are commonly used for diagnosing fractures, infections, and other medical conditions. X-Ray imaging works by passing a small amount of radiation through the body and capturing the radiation that exits on a detector, creating an image of the internal structures.

  • Detect fractures and broken bones
  • Identify infections and tumors
  • Examine the chest for lung conditions
  • Monitor the progression of certain diseases
  • Assess the alignment of bones and joints
